# The Pharmacy

A collective of pharmacist characters who break down health, wellness, supplements, and medication knowledge in short-form videos. Each pharmacist has a different specialty and personality.

## Pipeline

1. **Image** — Generate character photo with NanoBanana Pro
2. **Voice** — Generate voiceover with MiniMax or ElevenLabs
3. **Video** — Combine image + voice with InfiniteTalk (RunPod for cheaper, or official site for quick tests)

## Characters
