How We Work

From Prompt to Premiere

01

Concept & Vision

We start with your idea — a mood, a story, a feeling. Through creative sessions we define the visual language, narrative arc, and emotional tone of your project.

02

Script & Storyboard

AI-assisted scriptwriting and storyboarding bring your concept to life on paper. Every scene is mapped, every shot is intentional before we generate a single frame.

03

Generation & Curation

Using state-of-the-art AI tools, we generate hundreds of visual iterations. Our directors curate the best outputs — the human eye decides what makes the cut.

04

Post & Delivery

Professional editing, color grading, sound design, and final polish. The result is a cinematic piece indistinguishable from traditional production — at unprecedented speed.

About

The House

Prompt House is a production studio built for the AI era. We believe the next generation of cinema won't come from bigger budgets — it will come from bigger ideas.

Our team combines filmmaking instinct with technical mastery of generative AI. We don't replace human creativity — we amplify it. Every frame we produce passes through a human director's eye before it reaches yours.
